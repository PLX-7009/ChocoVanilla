## ✨ Visual Improvements

Minecraft is pretty. ChocoVanilla just politely reminds it to try a little harder. Mods like **Better Clouds**, **AmbientSounds**, **Visuality**, **Particle Rain**, **FancyBlockParticles**, and **LambDynamicLights** add richer weather, dynamic lighting, immersive sounds, and satisfying visual effects that make the world feel far more alive.

## 🌍 Enhanced World Generation

The world shouldn't feel the same after your tenth playthrough. **Geophilic**, **Blooming Biosphere**, **Dungeons and Taverns**, **Katters Structures**, and **Unnamed Sea** introduce better landscapes, new structures, and more reasons to wander off in the completely wrong direction.

## 🏗️ Building & Decoration

Whether you're building a cozy cottage or a castle you'll abandon halfway through, mods like **Concrete Slabs**, **Terracotta Shingles**, **Terracotta Stairs**, **Shelves**, **Pottery**, and **Visual Workbench** give you more creative freedom while staying true to the vanilla style.

## 🎮 Quality of Life

The little things matter. **AppleSkin**, **Jade**, **Right Click Harvest**, **Better Statistics Screen**, **Inventory Item Groups**, and **Pick Up Notifier** remove everyday annoyances so you can spend less time fighting the UI and more time fighting your nemesis.

## ⚡ Performance & Optimizations

Pretty doesn't have to mean laggy. **Sodium**, **Lithium**, **FerriteCore**, **ModernFix**, **ImmediatelyFast**, **EntityCulling**, and **Dynamic FPS** work behind the scenes to keep your game running smoothly while your GPU quietly questions its life choices.

## 🎥 Immersion & Animations

Small details make a huge difference. **FirstPerson**, **Better Third Person**, **CameraOverhaul**, **Fancy World Animations**, **ChatAnimation**, **Spawn Animations**, and **Presence Footsteps** make movement, combat, and exploration feel smoother and more immersive.
